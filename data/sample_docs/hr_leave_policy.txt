HR LEAVE AND ATTENDANCE POLICY
Acme Robotics Pvt. Ltd.

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year,
credited at 1.5 days per completed month of service. Unused annual leave
may be carried forward to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical
certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
Sick leave cannot be carried forward or encashed.

3. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the
first two children, and 12 weeks for subsequent children, in accordance
with the Maternity Benefit Act. Male employees are entitled to 15 days of
paid paternity leave, to be availed within 6 months of the child's birth.

4. WORK FROM HOME
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with prior manager
approval. Engineering and support teams on active incident rotations must
remain reachable during work-from-home days.

5. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
The company observes 12 public holidays per year, published annually by
the HR department in January.

6. UNPAID LEAVE
Employees who have exhausted their paid leave balance may apply for unpaid
leave of up to 30 days per year, subject to manager and HR approval.

7. RESIGNATION AND NOTICE PERIOD
Employees below the manager level must serve a notice period of 30 days.
Employees at the manager level and above must serve a notice period of
60 days. The company may, at its discretion, allow buyout of the notice
period.
