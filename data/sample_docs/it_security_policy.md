# IT Security and Data Protection Policy
**Acme Robotics Pvt. Ltd.**

## 1. Password Requirements
All employee accounts must use passwords that are at least 12 characters
long, contain a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols, and are
rotated every 90 days. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for
all systems that store customer data.

## 2. Data Classification
Company data is classified into three tiers:

- **Public** — may be shared externally without restriction (marketing
  material, published blog posts).
- **Internal** — for employee use only (internal wikis, meeting notes).
- **Confidential** — customer data, financial records, and source code.
  Confidential data must be encrypted at rest and in transit, and access
  must be logged.

## 3. Device Security
Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and an approved
endpoint protection agent installed before being issued to an employee.
Personal devices used to access company email must be enrolled in the
Mobile Device Management (MDM) system.

## 4. Incident Reporting
Any suspected security incident (phishing email, lost device, unauthorized
access) must be reported to security@acmerobotics.example within 1 hour
of discovery. The security team targets an initial response within 4
business hours for all reported incidents.

## 5. Third-Party Vendor Access
Vendors requiring access to internal systems must sign a Data Processing
Agreement (DPA) and undergo a security review before credentials are
issued. Vendor access is reviewed quarterly and revoked immediately upon
contract termination.

## 6. Data Retention
Customer data is retained for 5 years after the end of the customer
relationship, after which it is securely deleted, unless a longer period
is required by applicable law.
