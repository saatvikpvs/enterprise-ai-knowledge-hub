Frequently Asked Questions - Acme Delivery Robot (Model AR-200)

What is the maximum payload of the AR-200?

The AR-200 can carry a maximum payload of 25 kilograms across a single delivery run, distributed across up to 4 internal compartments.

What is the battery life?

On a full charge, the AR-200 operates for approximately 8 hours of continuous delivery operation, or roughly 40 kilometers of total travel distance, depending on terrain and payload.

What are the operating temperature limits?

The AR-200 is rated for operation between -10°C and 45°C. Operating outside this range may trigger automatic safety shutdown.

How is the robot serviced?

Routine maintenance is recommended every 500 operating hours, including tire inspection, sensor calibration, and firmware updates. Customers under an active Acme Care contract receive on-site servicing within 48 hours of a reported fault.

What warranty is included?

Every AR-200 unit ships with a standard 2-year hardware warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended 5-year warranties are available for enterprise fleet customers.
